Foodify.ai 🌯 🍔

🚧 🚧 Work in progress 🚧 🚧

Our goal is to develop an app which enable users to take/upload a photo of any indian food, and app will provide an information about it such as nutrition, origin, recipe etc.

Initially we started with some food images which are available online and added some dishes.

You can try our very simple webapp and model to identify foods now. It works for 30 foods so far.

We are daily updating our database. You can also contribute in our database by by uploading food images here .

Building a largest dataset will likely take a lots of time to collecte images of indian food.

Thus, we will really appritiate you help. You can help us to create world's largest Indian Food Dataset by uploading food images here .
